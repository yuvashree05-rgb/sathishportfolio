Welcome to my academic realm! As an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at K Ramakrishnan College of Technology, Trichy, I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of materials science. With over 10 years of academic and research experience, my journey has been dedicated to advancing the field of metal joining, with a particular emphasis on the fascinating domain of super alloys. My expertise encompasses not only the intricate techniques required for joining these high-performance materials but also a comprehensive understanding of the rigorous standards for testing and validating their structural integrity and properties. My current focus is on pioneering innovative joining techniques that enhance the resilience, durability, and efficiency of super alloys in demanding applications, from aerospace to advanced manufacturing. This research direction builds on my solid background in metallurgy, where I actively engage with a wide range of experimental methodologies and sophisticated simulation techniques. Through careful analysis and experimentation, I aim to solve complex engineering challenges that arise when working with super alloys, developing methods that can ultimately improve performance and reliability in critical applications.
A key aspect of my work involves rigorous adherence to and advancement of industry standards for testing metals, ensuring that every innovation is met with validated quality and reliability. My expertise in these standards allows me to not only conduct precise testing but also to mentor the next generation of engineers in understanding the importance of quality assurance and industry compliance.
I am deeply committed to both research and mentorship, inspired to guide students and collaborate with fellow researchers to advance knowledge in metal joining and materials testing. By combining hands-on experimentation with advanced simulations, I strive to foster a learning environment that empowers students to think critically, innovate, and develop solutions that address real-world engineering problems.
As I continue my journey, I am excited to contribute meaningful advancements to materials science, setting new benchmarks in metal joining and inspiring transformative changes in the field. Through my teaching, research, and collaborations, I hope to impact the industry positively and support the growth of knowledge and innovation in materials engineering.

Research Interests: Metallurgy, Microstructural evolution, Thermomechanical processing of steels, Numerical Modelling, Oxidation, High temperaature materials, Characterization tools (EBSD, XRD), Density Functional Theory (DFT), Phase field simulations, Cutting-edge research, Innovation, Leadership and management skills.
